## Lotto & Fundraising Terms – Castlebar Celtic FC

### 1. Introduction

Castlebar Celtic FC may organise fundraising activities including lotto draws, raffles, and events to support club development.

Participation in these activities constitutes acceptance of these terms.

---

### 2. Eligibility

* Participants must be at least 18 years of age
* Participants must comply with all applicable laws

---

### 3. Entry and Payment

* Entries must be submitted through approved channels
* Payments must be completed before entry is valid
* The club reserves the right to refuse or cancel entries

---

### 4. Draw Process

* Draws will be conducted in a fair and transparent manner
* Results will be published via official club channels

---

### 5. Prizes

* Prizes will be as advertised
* The club reserves the right to substitute prizes where necessary
* Winners will be notified using provided contact details

---

### 6. Refunds

* Entries are non-refundable except in cases where the event is cancelled

---

### 7. Liability

Castlebar Celtic FC is not responsible for:

* Lost, delayed, or incomplete entries
* Technical issues affecting participation

---

### 8. Use of Funds

Funds raised will be used to support club activities, facilities, and development.

---

### 9. Changes

The club reserves the right to amend these terms at any time.
