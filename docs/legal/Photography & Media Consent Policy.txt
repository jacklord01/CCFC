## Photography & Media Consent Policy – Castlebar Celtic FC

### 1. Introduction

Castlebar Celtic FC recognises the importance of celebrating achievements and promoting the club through photography and video, while ensuring the safety and privacy of all members, especially children.

---

### 2. Purpose of Media Use

Images and videos may be used for:

* Club website and social media
* Match reports and news articles
* Promotional materials

---

### 3. Consent Requirements

* Written consent will be obtained from parents/guardians before capturing or publishing images of children
* Adult members may provide consent for their own images

---

### 4. Safe Use of Images

The club will:

* Avoid naming children in images where possible
* Never publish personal details alongside images
* Ensure images are appropriate and respectful

---

### 5. Photography at Events

Parents and spectators:

* May take photos for personal use only
* Must not publish images of other children without consent
* Must follow any restrictions set by the club

---

### 6. Social Media

The club will:

* Use official channels for posting content
* Monitor and remove inappropriate content
* Ensure compliance with safeguarding guidelines

---

### 7. Withdrawal of Consent

Consent may be withdrawn at any time by contacting the club. The club will take reasonable steps to remove images where possible.

---

### 8. Breaches

Any misuse of images or breach of this policy may result in action by the club.
